**Kep1er** (pronounced as Kepler) is a 9 member girl group formed through the survival show Girls Planet 999. The group consists of **Yujin**, **Mashiro**, **Xiaoting**, **Chaehyun**, **Dayeon**, **Hikaru**, **Huening Bahiyyih**, **Youngeun**, and **Yeseo**. The group is managed by WAKEONE ENTERTAINMENT and Swing ENtertainment and will promote for 2 years and 6 months following their debut date.
